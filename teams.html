Our Journey

Arijeem Enterprises began as a small family business in Lagos, founded by Alhaja Ayoola Jumoke Arikewusola with a vision to provide reliable beverage distribution services.

Over the years, we've grown into a trusted name in beverage distribution, serving businesses and individuals across Nigeria. Our success is built on relationships, reliability, and a commitment to quality.

Today, as a Global Pepsi Partner, we manage a fleet of delivery vehicles, maintain strategic partnerships with major beverage manufacturers, and continue to expand our service areas while maintaining the personal touch that defines our brand.
